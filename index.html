My Story

I earned my master's degree in robotics from Northeastern University, where I focused on computer vision and autonomous systems. During my studies I joined Mercedes-Benz Research & Development North America as an HD Maps intern, building tools for generating high-definition maps. After graduation I became a product manager at Dassault Systèmes, guiding SolidWorks features and collaborating closely with engineering teams. My role recently evolved into leading AI initiatives for SolidWorks as the AI Product Manager. Earlier in my career I served as an Assistant Manager at Suzuki Motor Corporation, working on ADAS integrations. I enjoy applying my technical background in C++ and Python to shape innovative software products.
