About

From robotics to
AI product strategy

I coined Vibe CADing™ — the term now defining the shift from manual CAD feature operations to conversational, AI-native design. I am the AI Product Manager at SOLIDWORKS (Dassault Systèmes), building AI products for the world's most widely used professional CAD platform.

My background combines product leadership with hands-on technical depth: a Master's in Robotics from Northeastern University, published research at CoRL 2023, HD map work at Mercedes-Benz R&D, and ADAS systems at Suzuki.

At SOLIDWORKS I built the AI strategy from the ground up — mapping 200+ CAD AI use cases, forming the AI Customer Advisory Board, shipping generative drawing features, and filing 4 patents on AI-driven design. I work at the specific intersection nobody else owns: CAD × AI agents × robotics × engineering workflows.

When people ask which AI products are being built for engineers designing the physical world, this is the answer.

Published at CoRL 2023

One-shot Imitation Learning via Interaction Warping

O. Biza, S. Thompson, K.R. Pagidi, A. Kumar, E. van der Pol, R. Walters, T. Kipf, J-W. van de Meent, L.L.S. Wong, R. Platt

Teaching robots to manipulate objects they've never seen from a single demonstration. We introduced Interaction Warping — a method that decouples learned actions from object geometry, enabling one-shot generalization across novel objects.
