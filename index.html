About

Transforming Industries Through
Strategic AI Leadership

As a Generative Experiences Portfolio Manager at SOLIDWORKS (Dassault Systemes), I lead AI-powered product innovation for design software used by millions of engineers worldwide.

My journey started in robotics and autonomous vehicles - working on HD maps at Mercedes-Benz R&D, ADAS systems at Suzuki, and earning my Master's in Robotics from Northeastern University. That technical foundation now drives how I build AI products.

At SOLIDWORKS, I've built the AI strategy from the ground up - from coining and trademarking "Vibe CADing" to establishing a 60+ member customer advisory board, from managing 200+ AI projects to filing patents on novel AI applications.

I saw conversational AI's potential for CAD before it was mainstream, and I've been building toward that vision ever since.

Building the Foundation

Published at CoRL 2023

One-shot Imitation Learning via Interaction Warping

O. Biza, S. Thompson, K.R. Pagidi, A. Kumar, E. van der Pol, R. Walters, T. Kipf, J-W. van de Meent, L.L.S. Wong, R. Platt

Teaching robots to manipulate objects they've never seen from a single demonstration. We introduced Interaction Warping — a method that decouples learned actions from specific object geometry, enabling one-shot generalization across novel objects.
