Graduate Robotics Software Developer

I have a strong background in C++, Python, and various frameworks and libraries, including OpenCV and TensorFlow. I am skilled in machine learning and have experience in developing models using PyTorch, TensorFlow, and scikit-learn. In my educational and professional experiences, I have demonstrated strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work effectively in a team. I am committed to continuous learning and professional development, and I am excited to apply my skills and knowledge to the field of autonomous driving.
